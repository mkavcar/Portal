Join our User Group

Join our User Group in SharePoint by clicking on this link Cross Client Reporting User Group. When prompted please login using your GroupM credentials (the username and password that you use to login to your GroupM computer), then request access to the group when prompted. We will approve your access as soon as possible.

This group will provide you access to the following resources:

  • News and announcements (such as Data Source additions or upgrades)
  • Support materials and user manual
  • Discussion boards and tips for all available Reports and Data Sources
  • The ability to request new access to the Reports
